Ravioli

Description

A recipe for delicious ravioli, inspired by a visit to Colletta in Alpharetta.

Ingredients

  • 1 package ravioli
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cups water
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 5 tbsp pesto
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cube raw chicken breast; lightly season with salt & pepper
  2. Cook chicken breast in an oiled skillet until internal temperature reaches 165°F
  3. Cover skillet and remove from heat
  4. Bring 3 cups water to a boil in a large pot
  5. Add ravioli and olive oil to boiling water
  6. Cook ravioli for 3-5 minutes
  7. Remove ravioli from heat and strain
  8. Add ravioli and chicken breast into large pot
  9. Add pesto, salt and pepper to taste
  10. Serve warm
