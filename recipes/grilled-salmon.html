Grilled Salmon

Description

A recipe for my favorite meal: grilled salmon!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb filet of salmon
  • 2 cups liquid marinade
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Marinate salmon in a shallow glass baking dish at least 3 hours prior to cooking
  2. Preheat grill to 400°F
  3. Place salmon skin-down on an aluminum foil-lined grilling tray
  4. Lightly season salmon with salt and pepper
  5. Remove from grill at 12 minutes or when internal temperature reaches 135°F, whichever occurs first
  6. Allow salmon to set for 2 minutes to continue cooking to minimum safe internal temperature of 145°F
  7. With a large kitchen knife, slice salmon into 1.5 inch filets
  8. Serve warm with asparagus and rice
