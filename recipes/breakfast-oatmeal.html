Breakfast Oatmeal

Description

Start the day off right with a heart-healthy bowl of oatmeal.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 3/4 cup water or milk
  • Fresh blueberries, raspberries, and/or blackberries
  • 2 tsp local honey
  • 1/8 cup crushed walnuts

Steps

  1. Combine oats and water in a large cereal bowl
  2. Microwave for 2 minutes; stir
  3. Add an additional 30 seconds if liquid is not fully absorbed by oats
  4. Mix honey into oats
  5. Add fruit and walnuts
  6. Serve warm
